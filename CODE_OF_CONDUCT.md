# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Treat contributors with respect. Harassment, abuse, discrimination, and bad-faith
interaction are not acceptable.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent unless there is clear evidence otherwise.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or hateful language.
- Deliberate intimidation or disruption.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or block
participants whose behavior harms the project.
